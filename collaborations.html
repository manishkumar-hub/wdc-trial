IIT Guwahati

  • NIT Sikkim and IIT Guwahati have come closer on various spheres. The long informal relationship of two sister institutes from North East was materialised into strong academic bonding when IIT G and NIT Sikkim signed an MoU for research and development. The MoU provides various opportunities for the growing institute which includes:
  • Informal faculty exchange.
  • Assistance in conducting the laboratory examinations of B.Tech students. It is pertinent to mention here that bunch of students were given options for conducting their ECE, CSE and EEE in IIT G for last two years. We have been sending a group of excellent students to IIT G for academic exposure, laboratory classes and examinations.
  • IIT G has facilitated NIT Sikkim by providing access to the various placement drives. From the first batch of students of NIT Sikkim many of them have got their jobs by attending these placement drives at IIT G.
  • IIT G has also extended access to its library, laboratory and various other sections to the faculty members of NIT Sikkim for their academic pursuits.
  • There have been a strong collaboration at the higher up level of these sister institutes for various purposes
